Declining Professions in Jammu & Kashmir

Declining

Traditional Textile Handloom Worker

Automation and changing consumer preferences are reducing demand for traditional handloom products.

Alternative Paths:

  • Textile design with digital tools
  • E-commerce for handmade products
  • Textile quality management
Declining

Data Entry Clerk

Automation and AI are making manual data entry increasingly obsolete.

Alternative Paths:

  • Data analysis
  • Database administration
  • Business intelligence

Traditional Farming

Climate change and economic pressures are making traditional farming less viable.

Alternative Paths:

  • Organic farming
  • Agricultural technology
  • Food processing
