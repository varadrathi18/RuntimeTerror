Frequently Asked Questions

Is graduation necessary after 12th?

Graduation opens doors to government jobs, higher studies, and professional careers. While short-term courses help in skills, a degree adds long-term stability.

Which stream should I choose after 10th?

It depends on your interest. Science, Commerce, Arts, and Vocational streams each lead to unique opportunities. Our platform helps match your interest with future careers.

What support do government colleges provide?

Most government colleges offer affordable fees, scholarships, library and hostel facilities, and access to qualified teachers.
